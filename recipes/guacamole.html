Guacamole

Description

With this simple recipe, you'll learn how to make some guac that you'll just want to keep eating.

Follow rubor's cooking show for more easy recipes that will level up your cooking!

Ingredients

(For 2 people)

  • Large avocados, nice and soft but not overripe - 2
  • Fresh lime - 1/2
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional, to taste) - 30g
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the avocado across the length and remove the pit. Cube the meat while still in the peel and scoop out the cubes into your molcajete or bowl.
  2. Squeeze the lime into the bowl and mash the avocado and lime juice. (Do not use a blender. Use a fork or a pestle instead to achieve the desired beautifully chunky texture.)
  3. Add salt & pepper to taste
