Tacos

Description

With this simple recipe, you'll learn how to make the most succulent tacos you ever had in your life.

Follow rubor's cooking show for more easy recipes that will level up your cooking!

Ingredients

(For 2 people)

  • Ground beef - 400g
  • Small soft tacos - 8
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped - 30g
  • Large onion, chopped - 1
  • Hot sauce
  • Fresh lime -1 optional
  • A batch of fresh guac (click here for recipe)
  • Spices: salt, pepper, cumin, paprika, cayenne, cinnamon

Steps

  1. Fry the onion on medium heat until glazy and soft.
  2. Add the beef and continue frying until well browned. Add the spices to taste and fry for 2 more minutes.
  3. Heat the tacos in a pan for 30 seconds on each side, or lightly humidify them and microwave for 30 seconds.
  4. Splash a big spoon of guac in the taco and add the mixture of meat and onion. Drizzle some hot sauce on the meat and garnish with a pinch of fresh cilantro and a squeeze of fresh lime.
